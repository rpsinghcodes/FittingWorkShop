Work Shop

Rules For Safety

1.Save Yourself

  • Never work without shoes
  • Hair should not open in case of girl and not too long in case of boy
  • Don't Wear loose cloths
  • Work with full attention
  • Wear Helmet
  • Don't talk to other while working
  • Use gloves and googles
  • Work Station should always be neat and clean.

    • 2.Safety of Machines

  • Don't play with machines that you don't know about
  • Never put oil while machine is ON
  • Light supply should be proper.

    • 3.Product safety (while we are making product)
